Gladiolus

Part of the iris family (Iridaceae) and commonly known as “glads,” these lovely flowering plants are available in a multitude of colors and sizes. Gladioli typically reach between 2 and 5 feet in height!

Oemleria

Oemleria cerasiformis, a shrub commonly known as osoberry or Indian plum, is the sole species in genus Oemleria. Native to the Pacific coast and ranges of North America.

Globeflower

Like buttercups on steroids, globeflowers are stunning massed beside lakes, ponds, and streams. Elsewhere, they are perfectly suited to rain gardens and boggy areas, and they make a good showing.
